“To impart quality education to the students with a set of the global standards by developing their all round personality and to prepare them as young and dynamic engineering…” is our Moto.

The S.V. Group of institutions is sponsored by S.V. Educational Society established in 2006 by a group of concerned academicians and enterprising educationists. S.V. Group of institutions was established with a goal of promoting self knowledge, leadership responsibility and diversity among the students and transforms them into fully integrated individuals. The institutions aim at fostering technical and management education with right blend of Indian ethics & values and develop outstanding entrepreneurial talent capable of providing global business leadership.

The S.V. Group of institutions popularly known as SVGI quickly won the confidence of the parent community and the students to become one of the select destinations for future engineers. Soon the lamp fo knowledge began to spread the colorful light far and wide. The SVGI is one of the best institutes in the state which has more goals to achieve. We are very happy to note that the SVGI has reached many milestones in its path towards excellence.
